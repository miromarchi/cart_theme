About

Cuban Art Worldwide is a project by Maretti Editore realized in collaboration with the Republic of Cuba Pavilion at the 56th International Art Exhibition, La Biennale di Venezia 2015. It promotes Contemporary Cuban Art and offers an easy-to-use tool to find exhibitions displaying the work of Cuban artists around the world.
